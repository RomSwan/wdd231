Schwertmeister

Site Name: Schwertmeister

I chose "Schwertmeister" because it is German for "Swordmaster".
The logo can be seen on the far-left side of the header.

Site Purpose:

The purpose is to sell real, but unsharpened, swords. It will display the information for each sword. There will be a map and address so customers can take a look in person. If the customer wants the store to ship them a product, they can place a request.

Senarios (audience questions):

What kind of swords are sold?
How can I purchase one?
Where is the shop located?
Will they ship my order?

Colour Schema:

The colour schema is:
Oxford Blue - #051d36 (main)
Platinum - #eaebea (secondary)
Regent Grey - #8c99a4 (accent 1)
River Bed - #4a5968 (accent 2)
Fire Brick - #ab3428 (accent 3)

Platinum and Oxford Blue will be used most often in headers, while River Bed will be used as the background colour for the entire site. Accent colours will add visual variety. I chose Fire Brick as one of the accent colours to envoke association with fire and weapon forgery.

Typography:

The header font is MedievalSharp, while the rest of the document will be mostly in Raleway.
